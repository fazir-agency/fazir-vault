# Adipocyde — Competitor Intel

> **Niche:** Weight Loss
> **Marketplace:** Shopee / TikTok Shop
> **Last Updated:** 2026-06-11

## Overview
Adipocyde is a weight loss supplement competitor in the Malaysian market. Known for aggressive direct-response funnels with strong social proof and urgency tactics.

## Funnel Structure
- **Hook:** "Rugi kalau tak cuba" / "Habis stok cepat"
- **Problem Agitation:** Fat loss struggles, diet fails, "berat tak turun-turun"
- **Solution:** Product as easy fix — "makan je, tak perlu exercise"
- **Proof:** Before/after photos, testimonial count (ribuan dah cuba)
- **Urgency:** Limited stock, "harga naik esok", countdown timer
- **CTA:** Beli sekarang — COD available

## Key Observations
- Heavy on **social proof** — number of sold, reviews count
- **COD** sebagai trust signal utama
- **Mobile-first** landing pages
- **BM + English mix** hooks
- Pricing: Mid-range (RM50-100) — accessible, not too cheap

## Weaknesses
- Testimonial quality sometimes questionable (stock photos?)
- Landing page load speed inconsistent
- Funnel terlalu panjang — ada drop-off di mid funnel

## Winning Angles (that we can replicate/improve)
- Urgency + testimonial combo — kuat untuk马来西亚
- COD-first approach — turunkan barrier
- Before/after visual — proven conversion driver

## Notes
- Target audience: 25-45, women primarily, BM speakers
- Platform: Shopee affiliate links utama
- Competitor juga ada di TikTok Shop — video pendek before/after
